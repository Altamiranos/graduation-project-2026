Minimum lab environment:
- 1 control-plane node
- 2 worker nodes
- Control-plane: 2 vCPU, 2.5–3 GB RAM, 25–30 GB disk
- Workers: 1–2 vCPU, 2 GB RAM, 25–30 GB disk each

Recommended lab environment:
- Control-plane: 2 vCPU, 3–4 GB RAM, 30 GB disk
- Workers: 2 vCPU, 2–3 GB RAM, 30 GB disk each

For an 8 GB host machine, avoid allocating too much RAM to the VMs. Around 6–7 GB total VM memory is already close to the practical limit.
